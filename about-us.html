    Wen HAN 韩文 is the founder, chairman and CEO of Windrose EV trucks. He has a MBA from Stanford University and Bachelor's from Williams College, majoring in Math, Economics, and Spanish. He and his wife Jessie Jia are also proud donors of scholarships at Stanford University, Purdue University, and Kettering University. Wen was named by Forbes as one of top 100 Outstanding Chinese, and a member of Fortune Magazine's 40 under 40 list. Wen is also a director of the Windrose Giants Antwerp, a men's, women's, and youth's basketball club in the port city of Belgium.
