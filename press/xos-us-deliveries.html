    Windrose US Customer Deliveries Begin in May Through Xos

    Xos Trucks will import, sell and support Windrose R700 electric trucks across North America.

    Xos Trucks will import, sell and support the Windrose R700 electric truck across North America, with first customer deliveries beginning in May 2026.

    The partnership marks Windrose's entry into the North American market through an established commercial vehicle partner with nationwide service coverage.
