Port of Antwerp-Bruges Welcomes Windrose for European Assembly

Windrose establishes Windrose Park Antwerp along Noorderlaan — its first European electric truck assembly facility.

Location
Antwerp, Belgium
Assembly HQ
Europe
Address
Noorderlaan, Antwerp

Windrose has established Windrose Park Antwerp along the Noorderlaan at the Port of Antwerp-Bruges — its first European flagship site for truck assembly, sub-assembly, and distribution.

The facility marks a key step in Windrose's European manufacturing strategy, enabling local assembly and distribution of electric trucks across the continent.

The Port of Antwerp-Bruges is one of Europe's busiest logistics hubs, with direct connections to major freight corridors across the EU. Windrose's presence here reinforces its commitment to European customers and partners.
