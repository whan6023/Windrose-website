Cybersecurity Management System 网络安全管理体系

Windrose holds UN Regulation No. 155 type approval, confirming that its Cybersecurity Management System (CSMS) meets the requirements of WP.29 for the full vehicle cybersecurity lifecycle — from concept and design through production, operation, and decommissioning. 温德罗斯持有联合国第155号法规型式认证，确认其网络安全管理体系（CSMS）满足WP.29对整车网络安全全生命周期的要求——从概念设计到量产、运营直至报废。

Extended Emergency Response Hierarchy 扩展应急响应层级
  • T1 Anhui CSIRT — Daily Operations Group Detection, initial triage, first-hour containment. Primary for Anhui-entity vehicles. 安徽CSIRT — 日常运营组：检测、初步分类、首小时遏制
  • T2 Windrose Technology Antwerp NV — EU Coordinator Escalation gate for EU-fleet incidents. Coordinates with T1 and notifies Transportstyrelsen within 72 h of confirmed incident. 温德罗斯科技安特卫普有限公司——欧盟协调员：负责欧盟车队事件升级，72小时内通知Transportstyrelsen
