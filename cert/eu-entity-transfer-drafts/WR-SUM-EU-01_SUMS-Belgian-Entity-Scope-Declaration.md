## 1. Purpose
To declare that the Software Update Management System (SUMS) certified under UN Regulation No. 156 is extended to **Windrose Technology Antwerp NV** as the manufacturer named on the Swedish WVTA `e5*2018/858*00525`, and to record the EU-fleet responsibilities the Belgian entity assumes.

The WVTA remains Swedish (Transportstyrelsen, e5); only the manufacturer entity and the WMI (→ YCB) change.

## 3. EU-fleet responsibilities adopted
1. **Release authorisation** — the Belgian entity's SUMS Owner authorises every software-update campaign affecting EU-registered vehicles before rollout (Form F-01, WR-T0108-SUMS-001-01).
2. **RXSWIN governance** — the Belgian entity maintains the RXSWIN ledger (WR-T0108-SUMS-003-07) and the vehicle type-approval ledger (Form F-04), and notifies Transportstyrelsen of RXSWIN-affecting changes.
3. **User notification** — per WR-B05-SUMS-EU-02 (EU User-Notification Addendum), in EN/FR/NL for the Belgian market.
4. **Monitoring & emergency response** — per WR-EU-SUMS-MER-01.
