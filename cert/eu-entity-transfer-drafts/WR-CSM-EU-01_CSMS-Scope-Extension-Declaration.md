## 1. Purpose
This declaration extends the scope of Windrose's Cyber Security Management System (CSMS) — established and certified under UN Regulation No. 155 — to **Windrose Technology Antwerp NV** (the "Belgian entity"), so that the entity named as vehicle manufacturer on the Swedish Whole Vehicle Type Approval `e5*2018/858*00525` is covered by a current, audited CSMS.

The WVTA remains a Swedish approval issued by Transportstyrelsen (e5). This document does not transfer the approval to another authority; it records that the CSMS obligations of the named manufacturer are discharged by Windrose Technology Antwerp NV.

## 2. Scope of the extension
| Aspect | Before | After this declaration |
|---|---|---|
| Manufacturer named in WVTA field 0.5 | Anhui Windrose Holdings Co., Ltd. | Windrose Technology Antwerp NV |
| WMI (VIN field 0.3) | LKL / LH5 | YCB |
| CSMS certificate holder | Anhui entity | Windrose Technology Antwerp NV (re-issue of `CYPRUS*CERT*1032*00`) |
| Vehicle types covered | WH13 / R700 (N3 BEV/FCEV) | unchanged |
| Production sites | CHTC KINWIN (Nanjing) · Higer Bus (Suzhou) | unchanged — supervised under the Belgian entity's CSMS governance |

The technical content of the CSMS — threat analysis, risk treatment, verification, monitoring and incident response — is **unchanged**. What changes is the **governing entity, its accountable roles, and the EU-facing obligations** set out in §4–§5.

## 4. Accountability under the Belgian entity
1. **CSMS Owner / CISO** — appointed within Windrose Technology Antwerp NV; holds overall accountability for the CSMS, signs the management-review conclusions, and is the single point of contact to the technical service and to Transportstyrelsen for cybersecurity matters. *(See WR-CSM-01 organisation chart & role matrix, EU revision.)*
2. **Anhui engineering organisation** — retains responsibility for the technical execution of the lifecycle activities (TARA, design, V&V) as a supplier to the Belgian entity, under the interface agreement.
3. **Management review** — the Belgian entity's KPIs are added to the annual CSMS management review (WR-CS&SUP-02 series).

## 5. EU-facing obligations adopted by the Belgian entity
The Belgian entity adopts the following EU obligations, detailed in the referenced new documents:
- **Threat-intelligence monitoring** of EU sources and CSIRT feeds (WR-T0104-CSMS-007-EU).
- **Coordinated vulnerability disclosure** with an EU point of contact (WR-EU-CSMS-VDP-01).
- **Recall & field-action notification** to Transportstyrelsen and affected EU member-state authorities (WR-EU-CSMS-RFA-01).

## 6. Validity
This declaration takes effect on signature and remains valid until the re-issued CSMS certificate `CYPRUS*CERT*1032*00` naming Windrose Technology Antwerp NV is granted, at which point it is incorporated into WR-CSM rev. (next).
