## 1. Purpose
To define how Windrose Technology Antwerp NV informs EU users of software updates to WH13/R700 vehicles, in the languages required for the markets of registration — for Belgium **English, French and Dutch (EN/FR/NL)**.

## 2. What users are told (per update)
- Purpose and content of the update (functional / safety / cybersecurity).
- Whether action or consent is required, and the consequences of not updating.
- Expected duration and any vehicle unavailability during installation.
- For OTA updates: timing, ability to defer (where safe), and confirmation of success/failure.

## 3. Channels & timing
| Channel | Use | Timing |
|---|---|---|
| In-vehicle HMI (in-app) | Primary OTA consent & confirmation | At offer and on completion |
| Dealer / service network | Diagnostic / USB / EOL deliveries; assisted cases | Pre-notification before campaign |
| Written summary on request | Owners requesting records | Within 30 days of request |

## 4. Language matrix
| Market | Languages |
|---|---|
| Belgium | EN, FR, NL |
| Sweden (approval market) | EN, SV |
| Other EU markets of registration | EN + official language(s) of that market |

HMI strings, dealer notices and the written summary are maintained in these languages in the notification content library; translations reviewed before each release.

## 5. Consent & data protection
- Safety-related updates: a re-confirmation step is presented; where deferral is unsafe, the update is mandatory and the user is informed accordingly.
- Where notification involves personal data or automated decisions, GDPR Art. 13 information and Art. 22 safeguards apply; processing records held by the Belgian entity's DPO function.

## 6. Records
Per-campaign notification log (content, languages, channels, timestamps, consent outcomes) linked to Form F-01; retained ≥ 10 years.
