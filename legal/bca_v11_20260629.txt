    (i)   Each HCIC Class A Ordinary Share outstanding immediately prior to the HCIC Merger Effective Time (other than shares in respect of which HCIC Shareholder Redemptions have been elected) shall be converted into the right to receive one (1) share of Company Class B Common Stock.

The Exchange Ratio applies uniformly to all TopCo shares on a fully-diluted, as-converted-to-ordinary-share basis:


    Exchange Ratio  =  Pre-Money Equity Value  /  Reference Price  /  Total TopCo Fully Diluted Shares

  - Pre-Money Equity Value = $10.00 × Total TopCo Fully Diluted Shares

  Diluted Shares, the formula simplifies to:

    Exchange Ratio  =  1.0000  (exactly one PubCo share per TopCo share)

  This one-for-one exchange applies uniformly to all TopCo shares
  regardless of class or series.
