    Platform Commonality

    Global parts overlap across variants

    Each variant is homologated for its target market — but shares a high percentage of components with every other variant, minimising tooling investment and enabling global supply chain consolidation.

    Base \ Compared 🇪🇺 Europe 🇦🇺 Australia 🇨🇳 China 🇺🇸 US
    🇪🇺 Europe 100% 91% 80% 79%
    🇦🇺 Australia 89% 100% 76% 81%
    🇨🇳 China 83% 81% 100% 77%
    🇺🇸 US 79% 83% 74% 100%

    Values represent percentage of shared unique components (part numbers) between variants. Minimum 74%, average ~82% — enabling a single global supply chain and drastically reducing localisation cost compared to traditional market-specific platforms.
