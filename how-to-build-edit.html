    Simplified & localised final assembly

    Seven straightforward steps — any certified local partner, no specialist tooling required. Reduces logistics cost, creates local jobs, enables rapid market entry across 5 continents.

    STEP 01
    Bare Chassis
    The structural backbone — lightweight frame ready to receive all modules
    ›
    STEP 02
    Battery Pack
    High-voltage packs drop into the chassis floor, lowering centre of gravity
    ›
    STEP 03
    Drive Axle
    Proprietary electric drive axle — motor, gearbox and brakes in one unit
    ›
    STEP 04
    Wheels & Tyres
    Heavy-duty tyres fitted — chassis is now road-mobile
    ›
    STEP 05
    Cab
    Aerodynamic cab module mounted, housing driver controls and HVAC
    ›
    STEP 06
    Rear Integration
    Fifth-wheel, charging sockets, lighting and rear fairings fitted
    ›
    STEP 07
    Complete Truck
    Final inspection, software commissioning, ready to haul
